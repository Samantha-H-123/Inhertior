A tax preparer or filer collects financial records, calculates tax liabilities, and prepares files federal, state, and local tax returns for individuals and businesses. They identify deductions and credits to minimize tax liability and ensure compliance with tax laws, often working long hours during peak tax season. Filing taxes is the process of submitting a tax return like IRS Form 1040 to tax authorities to report income, calculate tax liability, and claim deductions or credits. It reconciles taxes withheld from paychecks with actual tax owed, resulting in a refund or a tax payment. The deadline is typically April 15th.
